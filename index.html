Inside the Lines

Help the world learn to park

Parking can be a real challenge. We're hoping to help the world a better place by holding people accountable for their bad parking.

There's no more need to wish you could yell and scream at the moron who parked his Hummer right against your driver-side door or to roll your eyes at the person who's taken up four parking spots in-front of the building. Simply snap a picture of their poor parking job with the license plate in the picture and hold them publicly accountable here, where the world can see.
